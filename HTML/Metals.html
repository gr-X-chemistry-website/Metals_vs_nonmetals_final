Metals

Elements like metals are generally lustrous, malleable, ductile, and good conductors of heat and electricity. Metals are also generally hard and have high melting points. They are sonorous, meaning they produce a ringing sound when struck. Most metals are solid at room temperature, except mercury. They tend to form positive ions by losing electrons during chemical reactions.
