17.Chlorine(Cl)

About Chlorine

  • Chlorine is a chemical element with the symbol Cl and atomic number 17.
  • It is a yellow-green gas at room temperature and is highly reactive, making it a strong oxidizing agent.

Uses

  • Chlorine's primary use is as a powerful disinfectant for water (drinking water, sewage, and pools), surfaces, and industrial waste, killing bacteria and viruses.
  • It is also essential in the manufacturing of plastics like PVC,pharmaceuticles ,dyes,textiles etc.
  • It can be used as an antiseptic, particularly in the healthcare industry.

Chlorine in Ancient India

  • Common salt and bleaching agents used in ancient textile industries
  • Element identified in India in colonial chemistry labs.
