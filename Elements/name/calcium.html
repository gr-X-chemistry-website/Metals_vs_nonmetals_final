20.Calcium(Ca)

About Calcium

  • While ancient Romans used limestone to create lime mortar, a practice also potentially known or similar in India for building purposes, this does not mean they worked with calcium metal itself.
  • It is the fifth most abundant element in Earth's crust and is essential for biological processes like bone health and nerve function.

Uses

  • Calcium is primarily in metallurgy and as a reducing agent
  • It's used to produce other metals like thorium and uranium, and as an alloying agent in metals like aluminum, beryllium, copper, lead, and magnesium.
  • In steelmaking, calcium is a deoxidizer, desulfurizer, and decarburizer, improving the metal's properties.

Calcium in Ancient India

  • Lime (chuna) used in construction (Harappan cities), betel chewing, and metallurgy.
