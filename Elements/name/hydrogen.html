1.Hydrogen(H)

About Hydrogen

  • Lightest Element
  • Used as fuel in rockets

Uses

  • Used in ISRO cryogenic rockets
  • Fusion occurs in the sun, which releases huge amounts of energy
  • National Green Hydrogen Mission has been implemented to generate green hydrogen

Hydrogen in Ancient India

  • Not isolated in ancient India, but production occurred during metal-acid reactions in alchemical labs (Rasashala) without knowing it was a separate element.
