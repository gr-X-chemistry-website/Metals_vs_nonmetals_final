22.Titanium(Ti)

About Titanium

  • Titanium is a chemical element with the symbol Ti and atomic number 22.
  • It is a lustrous, silver-colored transition metal known for its high strength-to-weight ratio, excellent corrosion resistance, and biocompatibility.
