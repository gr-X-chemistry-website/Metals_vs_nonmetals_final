25.Manganese(Mn)

About Manganese

  • It is a hard, brittle, silvery-white metal, often found in combination with iron.
  • It is crucial for both human health and various industrial applications, particularly in steelmaking.

Uses

  • It is used as an essential alloy in steel production to improve its strength and toughness.
  • Used in batteries as a component in dry cell cathodes
  • Used in chemicals to produce bleaching powder, fungicides, and disinfectants

Manganese in Ancient India

  • Ancient Indian artisans also harnessed manganese's oxidizing power, although its utility as a disinfectant or chemical reagent would likely have been a later discovery.
  • Manganese's role in Ancient India was primarily as a coloring agent and alloy for hardening iron and bronze.
