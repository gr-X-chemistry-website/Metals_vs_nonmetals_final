7.Nitrogen(N)

About Nitrogen

  • Consists of about 78% of air
  • Inert gas

Uses

  • Nitrogen fertilizers for agriculture
  • Food preservation
  • Used for cooling systems

Nitrogen in Ancient India

  • Not identified as a gas, but ancient Indian agriculture understood “air and compost” for soil fertility; saltpetre (potassium nitrate) production was a major medieval industry.
