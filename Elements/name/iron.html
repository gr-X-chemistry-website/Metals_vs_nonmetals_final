26.Iron(Fe)

About Iron

  • Iron is a strong, silvery-gray transition metal and chemical element.
  • It is the most abundant on Earth.

Uses

  • Iron's main use is in construction.
  • It is used as a critical nutrient in biological systems for humans and animals to transport oxygen.
  • Iron is a component in many magnets, including naturally occurring ones like magnetite.

Iron in Ancient India

  • Iron use in ancient India began around the late second millennium BCE (circa 1500-1200 BCE)
  • Though some evidence suggests even earlier dates, particularly from the Tamil Nadu region
