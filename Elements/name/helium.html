2.Helium(H)

About Helium

  • Inert Noble Gas
  • Has the lowest boiling point of any element

Uses

  • Used as a coolant
  • Helium mixed with oxygen is used by divers to prevent decompression sickness, allowing them to dive deeper.
  • Can change your voice when inhaled ;)

Helium in Ancient India

  • Not known in ancient times; first observed in India in solar spectroscopy studies during British colonial period after 1868.
