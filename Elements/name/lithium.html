3.Lithium(Li)

About Lithium

  • Key for rechargeable batteries
  • Added to glass to improve their heat resistance and reduce thermal expansion

Uses

  • Used in EV batteries
  • Used in mobile batteries
  • Used in solar cells

Lithium in Ancient India

  • Not recognized as an element in ancient India; lithium-bearing minerals (spodumene, lepidolite) occur in Bihar & Rajasthan but were identified in 20th century.
